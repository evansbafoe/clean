Our Values

Our Core Values

Customer First: We view the quality of our services through our customer’s eyes and understand that we have to constantly find solutions to improve the quality of service that will exceed our customer’s expectations.
One Team: We strive to treat every member of our team with respect and dignity no matter their position in the company. We also encourage our employees to do the same to achieve results beyond expectations.
Personal Accountability for Excellence: We understand that trust is earned so when we make a promise, we keep that promise by setting our standards high with regard to performance. At Richway, superior performance is not an expectation, it is a reality.
Integrity: We always want to act ethically, honestly and fairly in all situations and at all times.
